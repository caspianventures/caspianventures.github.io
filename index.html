How Airbnb Ratings Work

Welcome to our getaway! We hope you enjoy your stay in our cozy, nature-close retreat. Your feedback is incredibly important to us, and we want to ensure you understand how Airbnb ratings impact our ability to continue sharing this space with others.

Why Your Rating Matters

Airbnb's standards are very high. Airbnb requires us to maintain a rating of 4.7 and ratings below 4.3 are completely removed from the platform. This means even ratings of 4 can be detrimental.

By giving a 4-star rating, you are indicating that our space might not meet the standard for inclusion on Airbnb, which could prevent others from experiencing what you have.

What a 4-Star Rating Means

A 4-star rating indicates that you found the experience non-satisfactory. For Airbnb, this translates to a recommendation that this space should not be on the platform.

How You Can Help

If you had a great stay, please consider leaving a 5-star rating to support us. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

If there were any issues, we appreciate your feedback and the opportunity to address them.
